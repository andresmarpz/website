<span className="text-[12px]">Procrastination — the action of delaying or postponing something (indefinitely).</span>

Go and start that thing. We are often trapped within our thoughts and think, plan or speculate too much before committing.


For instance, before starting this blog, I’ve had a few failed attempts where I just lost motivation because I wanted to make it just right.


That will not happen. Just start somewhere and iterate.
